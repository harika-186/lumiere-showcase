Our Philosophy

Perfumery
as a
Private Art

Lumière was founded on a single conviction: that perfume is the only art form that cannot be witnessed — only experienced. We do not make fragrances for everyone. We make fragrances for the person who already knows what they want, but has not yet found it.

Our collaborations with independent perfumers are ungoverned by trends. Each composition begins with a precise emotional brief — a memory, a landscape, a specific kind of silence — and is refined until the concept has disappeared and only the feeling remains.
